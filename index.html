Meet Frankenvon!

I have been an artist for my entire life, choosing to focus on it solely for many years. I have a long way to go progress wise, as every artist does, but the chase never stops and it satisfies me. I mainly work with digital media, Clip Studio Paint is my go-to program for any kind of illustration and concept development. I also enjoy branching into physical sculpting and 3D modeling, as it helps me find inspiration in the times where it is hard for me to create.
Currently, I’m more focused in on several solo projects that help me evolve my own storytelling abilities, but when it comes to working with others I contribute ideas/art and reformatting for Holly G.T.’s comic series, The Verse.
